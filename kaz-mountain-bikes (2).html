Our Story

Mobility
Reimagined
for Georgia

KAZ Mountain Bikes was founded on a simple belief: every person in Georgia deserves access to reliable, affordable transportation. Named after the legendary Mount Kazbek — Georgia's iconic peak — we bring that same spirit of endurance and freedom to city streets and mountain trails alike.

We tackle real problems: the high cost of daily transport, lack of convenient mobility options, and the gap between urban and outdoor access. Our subscription and rental model puts a quality Salcano mountain bike in your hands — on your schedule, at your price.

Our Partners

Built on
Strong Partnerships

We don't ride alone. KAZ is backed by world-class partners who share our vision for accessible, quality cycling across Georgia.

Joint Venture
GeoRiders
Our joint venture partner in tourism and cross-country bike tours. GeoRiders connects KAZ subscribers with guided mountain tours, cultural routes, and adventure packages across Georgia's most stunning landscapes — from Tbilisi to Kazbegi and beyond.
Manufacturing Partner
Salcano
Istanbul-based bicycle manufacturer and one of Europe's leading bike producers. Salcano supplies KAZ with high-quality, durable mountain bikes engineered for urban commuting and rugged Georgian terrain — built to last, ride after ride.
